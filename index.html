Welcome!

I am an associate professor in the department EECS at IIT Bhilai. Earlier, I was an assistant professor of CSE at IIT Gandhinagar (2014-17). I obtained my BE, MTech and PhD degrees from Jadavpur University (1994 - 98), Indian Statistical Institute (1999 - 01) and KU Leuven (2001 - 06), respectively. I spent several years as a postdoctoral (or guest) researcher at KU Leuven (2006 - 08), NIST, USA (2008 - 12) and University of Waterloo (2012 - 14).

Since 2001, my research has always centered on cryptography and related areas.
