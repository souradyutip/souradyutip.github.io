Welcome to my personal homepage!








I am an associate professor in the department EECS of IIT Bhilai. Earlier, I worked as an assistant professor of CSE at IIT Gandhinagar (Jul. 2014 - Oct. 17). I obtained BE, MTech and PhD degrees, respectively, from Jadavpur University (1994 - 98), Indian Statistical Institute (1999 - 01) and KU Leuven (2001 - 06). I spent a long span of time (about 8 years) working as a postdoctoral (or guest) researcher at various well-known security research groups in Europe, USA, and Canada, namely, COSIC, KU Leuven (2006 - 08), CSD, NIST, USA (2008 - 12) and CrySP, University of Waterloo (2012 - 14).

Since 2001, my research has always centered on cryptography and related areas.
